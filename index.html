About Me

My name is Gillian Bayne, and I am a recent graduate of New York University, where I studied Integrated Design and Media at the Tandon School of Engineering and Game Design at the Tisch School of the Arts. My experience spans web design, UX/UI, game development, and technical media production.

As a Web Design Intern, I contributed to the redevelopment of the Phillipsburg School District website, refining its structure, enhancing user experience, and designing visual assets. During my study abroad semester in Sydney, Australia, I collaborated on wireframing an app for a startup using Figma, focusing on user engagement and workplace integration. Most recently, I worked at the NYU IDM Equipment Room, where I managed and maintained a range of technical equipment, including cameras, lighting, and VR headsets.

In addition to my design experience, I have a strong technical foundation in C#, Python, and JavaScript, alongside proficiency in Maya, Unity, and Unreal Engine. Leveraging these skills, I have developed games such as Jurassic Bird, Coffee Rush, and Mushroom World, showcasing my ability to merge creativity with technical execution.
