My name is Gillian and I am a recent graduate of New York University. I studied Integrated Design and Media at the Tandon School of Engineering and Game Design at the Tisch School of the Arts. I previously worked as a web design intern and helped develop Phillipsburg School District’s website. During my study abroad semester in Sydney, Australia, I helped wireframe an app for a startup using Figma. I was most recently employed at the NYU IDM Equipment Room, where I work with various technical equipment including cameras, lighting, VR headsets, etc. Additionally, I have experience with the coding languages, C#, Python, and JavaScript, and software including Maya, Unity, and Unreal. With this knowledge, I was able to create games including Jurassic Bird, Coffee Rush, and Mushroom World.
