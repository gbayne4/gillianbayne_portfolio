My name is Gillian and I am a recent graduate of New York University. I studied Integrated Design and Media at the Tandon School of Engineering and Game Design at the Tisch School of the Arts. I previously worked as a web design intern and helped develop Phillipsburg’s District website, in terms of creating assets and organizing the site. I also interned at the startup Co-Uspace in Sydney, Australia, where I helped wireframe an application to be partnered with their product, the smart desk. I am currently employed at the NYU IDM Equipment Room, where I work with a variety of technical equipment including cameras, lighting, VR headsets, etc. Additionally, I have experience with the coding languages, C#, Python, and JavaScript, and software including Maya, Unity, and Unreal. With this knowledge, I was able to create games including Jurassic Bird, Coffee Rush, and Mushroom World.
